# Writing Checklist

- Can the core idea be stated in one sentence?
- Does every section advance, complicate, or resolve that idea?
- Is every visual claim supported by reviewed media?
- Are factual gaps marked for confirmation?
- Does the opening create context and tension without deceptive clickbait?
- Does narration leave room for image and sound?
- Are sentence length, diction, and humor appropriate for the audience?
- Does the emotional curve vary rather than remain uniformly intense?
- Are titles accurate after the script is complete?
- Can the script work without TTS if the user declines it?
- Can every factual, explanatory, and emotional beat be mapped to existing
  visual or audible evidence?
- Are any deliberately required shots marked clearly enough for the final
  script-to-cut pickup audit?
